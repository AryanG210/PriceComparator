E-comparator

This project helps you to find a particular product from different websites

You can search for the product of your choice, and the website will show the top products from Amazon and Flipkart shopping pages.

This is a brief description of how the system works and what users can expect when using it.

Additional Features:

  • First, the user enters the homepage and searches for a product.
  • The website shows results from Amazon and Flipkart.
  • Here, the user can add products to his cart for later viewing or he can click on buy now to land on the webpage of that particular product.
